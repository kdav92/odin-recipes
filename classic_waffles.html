Classic Waffles

These aren't just your basic waffles

These are your grandma's homemade waffles made quick and easy.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups warm milk
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix together flour, salt, baking powder and sugar; set aside. Preheat waffle iron to disired temperature.
  2. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs. Stir in the milk, butter, and vanilla. Pour the milk mixture into the flour mixture; bear until blended.
  3. Ladle the batter intot a preheated waffle iron. Cook the waffles until golden and crisp. Serve immediately.
